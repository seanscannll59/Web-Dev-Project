Killarney's Historics Rally 2024

The Killarney Historics Rally is held on the last weekend in November every year. Pictures from this year's rendition can be viewed above. Pictured is a TRD-kitted Lexus IS200 (left), an Msport edition BMW E46 320CD (middle), and a facelift Toyota Corolla E11 saloon (right)

LZ World Tour Mondello

The LZ World Tour is a drifting event with multiple stages all over the world, one of which happens right in Ireland's own Mondello Park. The event doubles as a huge car show, in which hundreds of people are able to put their hundreds of hours of hard work and passion on show. Several cars from the event are shown above, including two Nissan Silvia S14s (left and right), and a highly modified BMW E36 saloon (center).
